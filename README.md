# Stakeholder Mapping

## Course

**Critical Thinking, Design Thinking, Leadership and Teamwork**

## Topic

**Design Thinking Practices for Technical Blog Writing**

## Task 09

**Stakeholder Mapping**

## Selected Project

**AI Study Assistant App**

---

## Primary Stakeholders

### Students

* Primary users of the application
* Use AI-powered features for studying, doubt-solving, note generation, and exam preparation
* **Interest:** High
* **Influence:** High

### Teachers and Educators

* Create and recommend learning materials
* Monitor student progress and provide guidance
* **Interest:** High
* **Influence:** Medium

---

## Secondary Stakeholders

### Educational Institutions

* Promote digital learning initiatives
* Support adoption of AI-based education tools
* **Interest:** Medium
* **Influence:** High

### App Development Team

* Design, develop, and maintain the platform
* Implement new features and fix issues
* **Interest:** High
* **Influence:** High

### AI and Machine Learning Engineers

* Develop recommendation systems and intelligent tutoring features
* Improve learning accuracy and personalization
* **Interest:** High
* **Influence:** High

### Cloud Service Providers

* Provide secure hosting, storage, and computing infrastructure
* Ensure application availability and scalability
* **Interest:** Medium
* **Influence:** Medium

### Cybersecurity Team

* Protect user data and maintain privacy
* Prevent cyber threats and unauthorized access
* **Interest:** High
* **Influence:** High

### Parents or Guardians

* Track students' learning progress and academic improvement
* Encourage effective study habits
* **Interest:** High
* **Influence:** Medium

### Investors and Business Partners

* Support business growth and product expansion
* Focus on profitability and market success
* **Interest:** Medium
* **Influence:** Medium

### Content Creators and Subject Experts

* Develop high-quality educational content
* Keep study materials updated and relevant
* **Interest:** Medium
* **Influence:** Medium

### Government and Education Authorities

* Ensure compliance with education policies and data protection regulations
* Encourage responsible use of AI in education
* **Interest:** Medium
* **Influence:** High

---

## Conclusion

The success of the **AI Study Assistant App** depends on effective collaboration among students, educators, developers, institutions, and regulatory bodies. Understanding stakeholder interests and influence helps create a user-centered, secure, and scalable learning platform that delivers personalized educational experiences.
